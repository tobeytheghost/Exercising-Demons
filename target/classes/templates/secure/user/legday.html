Leg Day

Workout Name Description Sets Reps Weight Status
Quad Extension Performed with a lever machine, where you sit on a padded seat and raise a padded bar with your legs.
Leg Press Using a leg press machine, push the platform away from your body by extending your knees. This targets the quadriceps, hamstrings, and glutes.
Hamstring Curl Using a machine, curl your legs towards your buttocks, targeting the hamstrings.
Calf Raises Stand on a raised surface and lift your heels by pushing through the balls of your feet. This targets the calf muscles.
Walking Lunges Take steps forward, lowering your hips toward the floor. This exercise targets the quadriceps, hamstrings, and glutes.
